Flagship product initiative

CivicLink

CivicLink is the flagship product initiative under Madison Avenue Designs. It is being developed as a governance-first AI platform focused on civic decision workflows, beginning with zoning and land-use.

Its current direction emphasizes deterministic-first logic, evidence-linked outputs, human review, modular workflow design, and traceable support for planning decisions.
