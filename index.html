Flagship product initiative

CivicLink

CivicLink is the flagship product initiative under Madison Avenue Designs. It is a governance-first AI platform focused on civic decision workflows, beginning with zoning and land-use.

The initiative emphasizes deterministic-first logic, evidence-linked outputs, bounded autonomy, human review, traceability, auditability, and responsible adoption.

Core principles

  • Evidence before interpretation
  • Human review before final decisions
  • Traceable outputs over black-box automation
  • Workflow modernization without replacing institutional judgment
