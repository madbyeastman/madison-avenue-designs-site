Flagship Product Initiative

CivicLink is the flagship product initiative under Madison Avenue Designs. It is a governance-first AI platform focused on civic decision workflows, beginning with zoning and land-use. Its direction emphasizes deterministic-first logic, evidence-linked outputs, human review, and traceable, auditable support for planning decisions.
